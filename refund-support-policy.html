Refund and Support Policy

The service uses scope-first payment to reduce refund risk and avoid unsafe or unsuitable work.

Before payment

Do not pay first. Email a public-safe scope request. If the request is out of scope or unsafe, it will be declined before payment.

Refund rule

If payment is made after written scope acceptance but the agreed audit deliverable is not started, a refund can be requested by email. If the agreed audit has been delivered, refunds are not automatic, but correction requests for factual errors or missing agreed fields are supported.

Support

Support is handled by email at binhaitjzhda@gmail.com. Include the public route URL, original scope, payment date if any, and the specific correction or support request. Do not include private credentials or payment data.

Correction standard

If the deliverable omits an agreed field or contains a verifiable factual error, we will correct the audit summary or provide a clarifying addendum.
