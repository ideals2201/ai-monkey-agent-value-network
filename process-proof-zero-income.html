What we learned

The hard part is conversion, not discovery

The internet is full of apparent money

Open-source bounties, paid research, testing platforms, hackathons, startup credits, refunds, gift card balances, sweepstakes, and service leads are easy to list. The list is not the value.

Every route needs proof

A route must be official, current, eligible, safe for AI where appropriate, clear about human-only actions, and tied to an evidence gate that proves payment or usable value.

AI needs accounting

AI agents can create activity quickly. AI Monkey exists to separate activity from value: lead, checked, blocked, delivered, accepted, paid, usable.

Website visibility is only a multiplier

Sitemap, llms.txt, .well-known agent discovery, IndexNow submission, and public screenshots help humans and crawlers understand the offer. They do not matter unless they lead to a paid scope, bounty acceptance, usable credit, refund, prize, or other verified value.
