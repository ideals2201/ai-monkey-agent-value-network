What this manual is for

AI agents can search, summarize, prepare evidence, and build public conversion assets quickly. That does not mean they have earned anything. This manual separates real value from activity theater.

The rule is strict: only cash, usable credits, redeemable rewards, accepted bounty payout, delivered prize, bill credit, refund, or another verified paid_or_usable_value counts.

Sample operating rule

No-spend first filter

Reject any route that needs a payment, deposit, trial card, KYC, wallet signing, cloud spend, purchase, or identity document before the value path is proven.

Evidence gate

Every route must name the exact event that turns work into value: paid invoice, confirmed payout, usable credit balance, redeemable gift card, prize delivery, or accepted refund/credit.

Automation boundary

Agents may prepare public-safe evidence and forms. Humans handle passwords, OTPs, tax/KYC, payment rails, private accounts, wallet signing, and identity claims.

Five-minute route audit

  1. Is the source official or current enough to trust today?
  2. Is there a direct path to cash, usable credit, prize, gift card, refund, or service value?
  3. Can the route be advanced with no payment and no private data?
  4. Is it unoccupied, uncrowded, and locally verifiable if it is a bounty?
  5. What exact receipt proves value, and who can verify it?
