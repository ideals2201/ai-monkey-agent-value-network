Privacy Policy

AI Monkey Agent Value Network provides public-source opportunity and value-route audits. The service is designed to avoid collecting private data.

Data we request

For the fixed USD 10 public-source audit, we ask buyers to send only public-safe scope information by email: a public URL, route name, current status, what should be checked, deadline/currentness, and any public proof URL.

Data we do not request

Do not send passwords, OTPs, API keys, private tokens, cookies, localStorage, private keys, seed phrases, KYC/tax/identity documents, bank/card data, wallet signing data, full private address, private phone, claim codes, gift-card codes, private screenshots, private dashboard text, or customer/third-party private data.

Use of information

Public-safe scope information is used only to evaluate the requested public route and produce a GO / HOLD / NO-GO audit deliverable. We may keep a public-safe receipt or summary for evidence-gated operations, but private or sensitive data should not be submitted.

Payment data

This website does not process card or bank details. Payment, when a scope is accepted, is routed through PayPal.Me. Do not email payment credentials or account data.
